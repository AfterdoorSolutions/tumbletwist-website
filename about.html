About Us

The Story Behind
TumbleTwist

We're a team of event enthusiasts on a mission to create extraordinary experiences for kids, schools, and corporates across Australia.

Our Journey

From a Small Idea to Big Adventures

TumbleTwist was born in 2020 from a simple belief: every child deserves access to well-organised, safe, and genuinely fun events. What started as community summer camps in Melbourne quickly grew into a full-service event company.

Today, we manage summer camps, school events, outdoor adventure tours, and corporate team building programs across Victoria — helping thousands of participants create lasting memories every year.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver safe, engaging, and high-quality event experiences that inspire joy, build confidence, and create meaningful connections for participants of all ages.

Our Vision

To become Australia's most trusted and innovative event management company for youth and corporate programs — setting new standards for creativity, safety, and fun.

What We Stand For

Core Values

Safety First

Every event is planned with safety at the core — from trained staff to risk assessments and first-aid readiness.

Creative Excellence

We bring fresh ideas and innovative themes to every event, ensuring no two experiences are the same.

Inclusivity

Our programs are designed so every child and adult can participate, regardless of ability or background.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices in all our events — from reducing waste to choosing sustainable venues.
